About

First what is dance? Dance is an art form that involes rhythmic movement of the body. Dance is loved by many. It can be enjoyed by all ages whether it is being watched or performed. It also comes in many forms. Some are quick and sharp while others can be smooth and flowy.

On this page there are many useful tools for dance. Whether you're a beginner or have been a dancer for a long time there is something on this page for everyone.

Dance has been around for a long time. It has been changed prefected by many people over the years. Here you can learn a breif history of dance. How it began. How it has changed and what it is today.

This page also includes tips on how to teach dance with lesson plans and videos. There's information for new teachers and teachers who have been teaching for awhile. There is also links included to the best online dance attire webpages so that you can get the best deals. We have also provided work and tips from well known choreographers from around the world.

Martha Graham said it best when she said,

"Nobody cares if you can't dance well. Just get up and dance. Great dancers aren't made because of their technique but because of their passion."

We hope you find everything you are looking for.
Happy Dancing!
